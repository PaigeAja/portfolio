Justin Pinder Car Rental

Branding

The owners of Justin Pinder Car Rental were looking to revamp their brand, complete with island inspired business cards to match their island inspired website.

The Process

In 2019, the owners of Justin Pinder Car Rental tasked us with the opportunity to redesign their current (at the time) business cards. The first order of business was to gain a deeper understanding of the emotions we wished to evoke in the target audience. From here we had to develop a colour palette that matched those emotions, these colour not only had to work for the business cards but also for the new website. We worked closely alongside the owners of Justin Pinder Car Rental to pinpoint the features that they deemed necessary to create their new brand.
