Sea La Vie | Vacation Rental

Branding

The owners of Sea La Vie | Vacation Rental were looking for a brand identity that matched the laid back, island feel of their rental.

The Process

In 2019 the owners of Sea La Vie | Vacation Rental, approached us with the task of creating an identity for their brand. The key aspect was to have a colour palette inspired by the island of Eleuthera itself; this included hues of blue and yellow and coral. We decided to take it a step furhter and add the famous Eleuthera pineapple in the silhoutte, this also ties in with the look of the Welcome cards, as well as the Welcome and Guest books for the rental.
