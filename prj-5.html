Saint Life Productions

Branding

The owners of Saint Life Productions were looking to design a brand presence that captured the essence of their company.

The Process

In 2019, the owners of Saint Life Productions to create their brand identity this included business cards, as well as a new YouTube banner. The first order of business was to create a colour palette, the owners wanted their brand to evoke following emotions:
Growth
Reliability
Ambition
Sophistication
To achieve this we used hues of green, black and white, as well as the fonts Tuesday Night and Cinzel to create a strong, visually appealing identity for the brand.
